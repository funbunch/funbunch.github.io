Search

Integrating effective search functions used to be a major headache. CrownPeak Search is completely configurable and painless to embed into any website structure, making even the most complex websites easy to navigate.

CrownPeak Search can serve up search results for known and anonymous visitors. The entire experience is transparent, as visitors see content driven solely by their activity, navigation and other third-party data, in additional to any search term they might have entered into a traditional search dialog box

  • Faceted Navigation
    Improve the ability for customers to find products in a catalog.
  • Forced Results
    Prioritize and control results for the most important searches.
  • Related Articles
    Automatically generate links to related articles from within the site or across multiple sites.
  • Embedded Search
    Capture a specific search result and embed that result in any page on the site.
  • Targeting & Personalization
    Embed search results or forced results to different visitors.
  • Multi-Site Search
    Search results can be returned from multiple sites simultaneously, allowing for aggregation of large content sources
  • Section & Category Search (Advanced Search)
    Search only relevant sections of your site(s), sort those results in priority, or automatically cluster those results in dedicated pages or indexes.
  • Customized Results
    Return unique titles and/or descriptions that don’t necessarily match the title of the document.
  • In-Context Results
    Search results can be presented in context, with the search term highlighted in both the search results and in the selected target document
  • Alternate Spelling
    Search uses your site as a dictionary and suggests alternate queries.
  • PDF & MS Office Documents Search
    Return relevant results based on a full text search of dozens of document types, and indicate results with the appropriate icons.
  • XML & JSON Results Return
    Return results in XML, RSS-formatted XML, HTML, JSON, or any other standard format for parsing and insertion into a specific page or database.
  • Password-Protected Sites Search
    Search protected areas of sites, including subscription sites, extranets or intranets.
  • Security Management
    Deliver results only to visitors who have permission to see them.
  • Scheduled Crawling
    Set a schedule and pace for crawling or manually index your site at any time.
  • Control Over Indexing Timing & Depth
    Define how fast and how deep to scan your web server.
  • Multiple Domains Indexing
    Index as many domains as you want including mixed environments by file formats, content types, and databases, including dynamic, internal and external content
  • Comprehensive Reporting
    Identify successful indexes, pages dropped, broken links, dynamic links with easy to use color-coded reports
  • Guaranteed Speed
    Deliver relevant results back in sub-second times - guaranteed.
  • Meta Data Creation & Query
    Create specific metadata for your site, and provide relevant content on individual pages.
  • Automated E-Commerce Links
    Automate the process of relating products to specific catalog pages including e-commerce links from partner sites.
  • Content Syndication
    Push content widgets out to partner web sites.

Localized Marketing

Global companies need to market locally. With CrownPeak, it’s possible to maintain global brand standards and consistency while allowing local groups to create, launch and manage their own web and mobile sites, campaigns and social media content.

CrownPeak comes complete with an integrated translation management framework for multi-site and multi-language management. This framework allows content managers to identify a primary language and link it to one or more subordinate languages

When content in the primary language is modified, content owners of the subordinate languages are automatically notified so that they can make the necessary adjustments. These steps may include manual delegation of translation to a third party or leveraging one or more integrations to third-party translation management services.

Regardless of the translation strategy, CrownPeak ensures content is continually managed within the defined workflow.

  • Full translation workflow, including independent content mappings
  • Model sites for regions or products to enable rapid rollout
  • Shared content or functional modules for regional marketers
  • Control of core brand elements to give regional marketers freedom to market locally
  • Infrastructure that immediately moves local content to local delivery (CDN + global dynamic deployment)
  • Global end-user support and services 24/7/365
