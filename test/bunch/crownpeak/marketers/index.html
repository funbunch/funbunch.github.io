CrownPeak's SaaS-based architecture separates marketing-driven elements that drive the digital experience into their own secure and manageable environment, called the Digital Experience Layer™. This Digital Experience Layer consists of marketing content, look and feel, personalization, localization and mobile optimization.

Managing the Digital Experience Layer™ as a separate function enables organizations to launch new web and mobile projects and campaigns fast, without any IT effort. Marketing can implement CrownPeak directly and gain full control over the entire digital experience, often for less than the cost of having an agency do a small campaign.

  • Unleash multiple agency resources with a single interoperable platform
  • Deliver and manage content across multiple channels
  • "Wrap" IT-managed portals and applications with personalized marketing content

Enhance or replace legacy systems quickly and elegantly

Most on-premise web content management systems are too inflexible and slow to meet the needs of today's marketers. Unfortunately migrating from one on-premise solution to another can take up to a year. CrownPeak is the only platform that offers multiple solutions for solving this problem quickly and elegantly:

  • Replace existing systems in a fraction of the time required by other vendors
  • Leave content in place and enhance existing systems with CrownPeak's capabilities
  • Use CrownPeak for urgent projects and gradually migrate site content to the CrownPeak platform
Toggle Content Request Demo

Most global companies have web content management platforms in place. However, few are satisfied with the speed and flexibility of these systems and their features. These platforms are typically managed by budget-strapped IT departments that don’t have the resources to keep up with marketing needs.

CrownPeak can completely replace existing enterprise web content management infrastructures, while minimizing – often even eliminating – the migration process. Alternately, CrownPeak can "wrap" marketing-driven digital experiences around core business functions like e-commerce, support, look-up, quotes or even existing web content management systems

For organizations that require centrally managed content, CrownPeak can serve the central content repository or run side-by-side with systems such as Vignette, Adobe, Tridion, Oracle, EMC and Interwoven. This allows digital marketers to get critical projects live while leaving existing sites in existing systems. This can extend the life of current CMS implementations while enabling digital marketers to deliver improved experiences to website visitors.

Tools for Today’s Digital Marketer

CrownPeak gives marketers an arsenal of powerful tools to support the most sophisticated digital campaigns.
Content Authoring & Layout

Create sophisticated websites, landing pages, micro sites, mobile sites and more with an intuitive authoring and editing platform that, once setup, anyone can use - no coding required.

Integrated Campaign Management

Go beyond email to reach your customers and prospects. Create, launch and manage multichannel marketing campaigns across web, mobile, social media, kiosks, ATMs and whatever other channels you use.

Media Asset Management

Access all your digital marketing assets (like images, text, video, creative designs, etc.) in one central place, and easily leverage them across all channels. Shared permission-based access gives you the flexibility and control to maintain brand consistency regardless of format or location.

Localized Marketing

Build localized websites that deliver a great user experience with languages, images, products and offers that are specific to a country or region, while keeping the overall brand experience consistent across the globe.

Personalization

Identify target segments, compile relevant content and deliver it via multiple channels - smartphone, tablet, website, and kiosks. Use event triggered marketing (if X happens, then deliver Y content) to personlaize the website experience using a variety of available data.

Analytics

Make better decisions with integrated best-of-breed analytics. In a single dahsboard, you get targeting and testing results, social media analytics, conversion results and key data that is integrated from anyrelevant 3rd party system. The result is a clear picture of what’s happening, what’s working and what’s not.
