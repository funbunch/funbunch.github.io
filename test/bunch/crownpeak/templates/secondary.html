Development

The CrownPeak Platform allows developers to build digital experiences rapidly, using configurable templates and powerful, flexible, secure APIs, familiar to any .Net or Java programmer. As a pure SaaS solution, there is no set up and no complex development environment to maintain. Just log in, point your favorite IDE at the CMS Platform and start developing. Developers are more productive as a result and are able to focus on pure value-added customer experience management because database calls, memory management and capacity planning are out and real-time, interactive, inline preview is in.

Deployment

CrownPeak’s SaaS-based architecture means that hundreds of projects can be implemented in parallel. Organizations with dozens of different products or regional teams, can rapidly develop projects in parallel and release on their own, independent timeline. The CrownPeak Platform’s sophisticated security and access control model provides a straightforward way to maintain control over brand integrity, workflow or other elements where compliance is a desire or requirement.
